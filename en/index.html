What makes MetrouBUC different

Built for locals, travelers and students

Every feature is built to make the chaos of Bucharest’s subway a little easier to handle (and less annoying).

Signature Feature
Platform & Door Side Guide

No more guessing. MetrouBUC tells you exactly which platform to use and which side the doors open on — so you can board, exit, and transfer without slowing down.

Interactive Metro Map

Zoomable map of all 5 lines. Tap any station to see connections, exits and nearby places. Fully offline.

Smart Route Planner

Pick any two stations and get the fastest route with transfers and travel time instantly. No internet needed.

Real-Time GPS Tracking

Follow your journey underground and get alerted before you reach your station — even without signal.

Offline Schedules

Nearby Places

Frequently asked questions

Common Questions

Where can I find the Metrou București map?

The app gives you direct access to routes, station details, next train arrivals, nearby places and airport connections. You can also browse the web map at metroubucuresti.ro/map.html.

How many metro lines does Bucharest have?

Bucharest has 5 active metro lines: M1 (gold), M2 (blue), M3 (red), M4 (green) and M5 (orange), covering approximately 78 km across 63 stations.

Is the app free?

Yes, completely free on Android. No in-app purchases, no account required. Works 100% offline.

Does it work without internet?

Yes, 100% offline. Route planning, schedules, GPS tracking, accessibility info and station details all work underground without mobile data.

Is this the official Metrorex app?

MetrouBUC is an independent app, unaffiliated with Metrorex S.A. It uses publicly available transport data to provide free, offline navigation.

What are the metro operating hours?

Metrou București runs daily from approximately 05:00 to 23:00. Exact departure times for every station are available offline in the app.

Is there a metro to Bucharest airport?

Not yet. The future M6 line linking the city to Henri Coandă Airport is under construction, estimated for ~2029. MetrouBUC shows the closest bus and train connections.

How do I plan a route?

Open the route planner, select departure and destination, and the app instantly shows the fastest route with transfers and estimated time — all offline.
